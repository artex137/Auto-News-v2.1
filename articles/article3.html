They are a manifestation of the Earth’s own energy systems, revealing that we exist in an electric world. Yet, instead of harnessing these currents for the collective good, capitalist enterprises would rather exploit finite resources for profit, all while exacerbating environmental degradation. The truth is that telluric currents could be a game-changing energy source if only we could pry it out of the clutches of capitalists who want to keep the majority of the power—quite literally—dispersed amongst the elite few. ### The Corporate Monopoly on Energy Why, you might ask, does the big energy sector resist tapping into alternative sources like telluric currents?
